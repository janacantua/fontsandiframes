SOUTHWEST ELIXIRS:
YOU DESERVE WELLNESS

Photos and Words
by Jana Cantua

Southwest Elixirs believes firmly that wellness is deserved by everyone.
Their storefront, full of pink clay-like walls and beautiful green plants, makes you feel like you have entered a place of relaxation. And that's exactly
the message they want to convey. Since their early days working the food truck off of highway-35, owners Madi and Santiago Ortega have
passionately developed a menu that's not just good for your body, but that tastes like healthy food was intended to. We chose them as our feature
this month after tasting their full menu and realizing that you can't go wrong with what you order there. Their mostly locally-sourced menu and the
way that both chefs combine flavor will leave you feeling good inside and out.
Well deserved, they say.
