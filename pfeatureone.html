TILLAGE:
WHERE PLANTS ARE A WAY OF LIFE

Photos and Words by Jana Cantua

Tillage carries over 400 plant varieties. Their plants are thriving and when you purchase something from them, they have an extensive knowledge of
how to care for your new plant additions. They are a local favorite not only for the beautiful condition in which they keep their plants, but also for the well-designed pottery you can find in their store, some of which is designed by local artists. Pictured below is one of their plants and pots (left photo). Their store is properous, bold and calming. We recommend stopping by if you're looking to fill your home with something lush and vibrant.
