About Us

Quedate means stay in Spanish. The name came after realizing that although we were not born in San Antonio, this is the city we call home. We think that even if you're shopping by, you'll be impressed with the way a visit to our city will make you feel like staying a little longer. We truly believe that our community has so much to offer. And we know it can be time-consuming to find local places to eat, shop and experience so we created a curated place to find some of the city's talent. No fees, no advertisers. We just want to share the goods.
